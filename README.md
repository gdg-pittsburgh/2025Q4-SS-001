## Event Details

**Date:** Nov 6, 4:00 to 4:35 PM (EST)  
**RSVP'd:** 31 participants

**Showed:**8 participants

Join us for an exciting 30 minute rundown about the GDG Pittsburgh group goals, direction and upcoming events. We look forward to meeting you, creating networking opportunities and learning what the community wants to share for future events. An opportunity to share your ideas for future events while claiming your space in the community. Don't miss the opportunity to enhance your skills and grow your network in the tech, business and educational community.

> **Add Value, Solve Problems, Make Introductions.**
